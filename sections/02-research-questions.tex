\section{Research Questions}

\textbf{RQ1.} Can a groundwater-coupled VIC Denmark, calibrated and constrained by joint assimilation of \textbf{GRACE FO} terrestrial water storage, \textbf{SMAP} and \textbf{ESA CCI} soil moisture, streamflow, and Jupiter piezometric heads, reproduce the propagation of meteorological, agricultural, hydrological, and groundwater drought signals at national scale better than uncoupled or non-assimilated baselines?

\textbf{RQ2.} Does a hybrid framework, combining differentiable parameter learning for VIC parameters with \textbf{LSTM} or Transformer post-processing trained on Catchment Attributes and Meteorology for Large-sample Studies, Denmark (\textbf{CAMELS DK}), recover predictive skill in groundwater-dominated Danish catchments, where pure LSTMs underperform (Liu \textit{et al.}\nobreakspace 2024)?

\textbf{RQ3.} Can a probabilistic four-pillar combined index (Standardised Precipitation Index (\textbf{SPI})/Standardised Precipitation-Evapotranspiration Index (\textbf{SPEI}) plus Standardised Soil Moisture Index (\textbf{SSMI}) plus Standardised Streamflow Index (\textbf{SSI}) plus Standardised Groundwater Index (\textbf{SGI})), aggregated via copulas with full ensemble and posterior uncertainty propagation, classify drought severity more skilfully than \textbf{EDO CDI v4}, and improve seasonal predictability when forced with bias-corrected Seasonal Forecasting System 5 (\textbf{SEAS5}) and Artificial Intelligence Forecasting System Ensemble (\textbf{AIFS ENS}) or GenCast?
