\section{Approach and Work Packages}

The project is organised into five interconnected work packages following a de-risked sequence.

\begin{itemize}

\item \textbf{WP1: VIC Denmark with groundwater coupling.}
National VIC\,5 at 1--10\nobreakspace km, parameterised from \textbf{VICGlobal} and refined with Danish hydrostratigraphy from \textbf{GEUS} and Jupiter wells. The groundwater module follows \textbf{SIMGM} (Niu \textit{et al.}\nobreakspace 2007), with a staged escalation path to 2D lateral coupling (Zhang and Liang 2021) if validation justifies it.

\item \textbf{WP2: Joint calibration and multi-source data assimilation.}
Local Ensemble Transform Kalman Filter (\textbf{LETKF}) and Constrained Bayesian (\textbf{ConBay}) \textbf{MCMC} assimilating \textbf{GRACE FO} terrestrial water storage (sub-monthly L1B, Retegui-Schiettekatte \textit{et al.}\nobreakspace 2025), \textbf{SMAP} and \textbf{ESA CCI} soil moisture (De Lannoy and Reichle 2016), streamflow, and Jupiter piezometric heads. Built on \textbf{PyGLDA} (Yang \textit{et al.}\nobreakspace 2025) with Fennoscandian Glacial Isostatic Adjustment (\textbf{GIA}) correction via ICE-6G\_D.

\item \textbf{WP3: Hybrid extension via differentiable hydrology.}
Differentiable VIC trained via differentiable Parameter Learning (\textbf{dPL}, Tsai \textit{et al.}\nobreakspace 2021; Feng \textit{et al.}\nobreakspace 2022) on \textbf{CAMELS DK} (Liu \textit{et al.}\nobreakspace 2025), replacing per-basin calibration with gradient-based learning across 3,330 basins. An \textbf{LSTM} residual post-processor corrects biases in groundwater-dominated catchments (Liu \textit{et al.}\nobreakspace 2024).

\item \textbf{WP4: Probabilistic four-pillar drought index.}
Standardised sub-indices spanning the full propagation chain --- \textbf{SPI}/\textbf{SPEI}, \textbf{SSMI}, \textbf{SSI}, \textbf{SGI} --- aggregated via copulas into a probabilistic Multi-pillar Combined Drought Index (\textbf{MCDI}) with full posterior probabilities of drought severity categories.

\item \textbf{WP5: Short- to seasonal-scale forecasting.}
VIC DK forced with \textbf{SEAS5} and \textbf{AIFS ENS} or GenCast (Price \textit{et al.}\nobreakspace 2025) for 1--6\nobreakspace month outlooks, evaluated via Continuous Ranked Probability Score (\textbf{CRPS}), Brier score, and ROC, with reverse Ensemble Streamflow Prediction (\textbf{ESP}) diagnostics. Benchmarks: \textbf{EFAS Seasonal} and \textbf{EDgE}.

\end{itemize}
