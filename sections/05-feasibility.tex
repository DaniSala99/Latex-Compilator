\section{Feasibility, Fit and Outputs}

The plan is feasible within three years because Year 1 already delivers a publishable physics-based baseline, even if the hybrid extensions slip. The \textbf{AAU PyGLDA}, \textbf{ConBay}, and \textbf{HPC Claaudia} infrastructure provide a strong starting point, while \textbf{CAMELS DK}, Jupiter, \textbf{DMI Klimagrid}, and \textbf{HIPRAD} supply the data backbone. The project aligns with the Villum Young Investigator Programme on ``Synergy of Physics-based and Data-driven Methods'' and complements \textbf{GEUS DK model} expertise, opening natural collaboration with Henriksen, Stisen, and Schneider.
